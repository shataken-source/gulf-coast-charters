## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account
1. Visit gulfcoastcharters.com/apply-captain
2. Fill out the application form with your details
3. Upload required documents (USCG License, Insurance, etc.)
4. Wait for admin approval (typically 24-48 hours)
5. Receive email notification when approved

### First Login
- Use your email and password to log in
- Enable 2-factor authentication for security
- Complete your captain profile
- Set up your availability calendar

## Document Management

### Supported Document Types
- **USCG License** - Required for all captains
- **Insurance Policy** - Liability insurance required
- **Medical Certificate** - Current medical certification
- **CPR/First Aid** - Safety certifications
- **Background Check** - Criminal background check
- **Drug Test Results** - Required annually
- **Boat Registration** - For your vessels
- **Safety Equipment Certificates** - Life rafts, EPIRBs, etc.
- **Business License** - If operating as business
- **Tax Documents** - W-9 or tax ID
- **References** - Professional references
- **Additional Certifications** - Any other relevant certs

### Uploading Documents
1. Go to Captain Dashboard → Documents
2. Click "Upload New Document"
3. Select document type from dropdown
4. Choose file (PDF, JPG, PNG up to 10MB)
5. Add expiration date if applicable
6. Click "Upload"

### OCR Auto-Fill Feature
- When you upload a document, our OCR system automatically reads it
- License numbers, expiration dates, and other data are extracted
- Review and confirm the auto-filled information
- Edit any incorrect data before saving

### Editing Documents
1. Find the document in your list
2. Click the "Edit" button
3. Update information or replace file
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Deleting Documents
1. Find the document you want to remove
2. Click the "Delete" button
3. Confirm deletion (this cannot be undone)

### Emailing Documents
1. Select the document you want to send
2. Click "Email Document"
3. Enter recipient email address
4. Add optional message
5. Click "Send" - recipient receives secure download link

### Renewal Reminders
- Automatic email reminders sent at: 30, 14, 7, 3, and 1 day before expiration
- Push notifications if enabled (see Settings)
- Dashboard shows expiring documents with red warning badges
- Update documents before expiration to maintain active status

## Booking Management

### Viewing Bookings
- **Calendar View**: See all bookings on interactive calendar
- **List View**: Detailed list with filters
- **Mobile View**: Optimized for phones and tablets

### Accepting Bookings
1. Receive notification of new booking request
2. Review customer details and trip information
3. Check weather forecast for the date
4. Click "Accept" or "Decline"
5. Customer is notified immediately

### Managing Bookings
- **Modify**: Change date/time with customer approval
- **Cancel**: Cancel with reason (affects your rating)
- **Complete**: Mark as completed after trip
- **Add Notes**: Internal notes about the trip

### Availability Calendar
1. Go to Dashboard → Availability
2. Click dates to toggle available/unavailable
3. Set recurring availability (e.g., weekends only)
4. Block out vacation or maintenance periods
5. Changes sync immediately to booking system

## Mobile App Features

### Progressive Web App (PWA)
- Install on your phone like a native app
- Works offline with cached data
- Push notifications
- Fast loading times
- No app store required

### Installing the PWA
**iPhone/iPad:**
1. Open Safari and visit gulfcoastcharters.com
2. Tap the Share button
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"

**Android:**
1. Open Chrome and visit gulfcoastcharters.com
2. Tap the menu (three dots)
3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
4. Tap "Add"

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does approval take?**
A: Typically 24-48 hours after submitting all required documents.

**Q: What if my license expires?**
A: You'll receive reminders 30 days before. Upload your renewed license immediately to avoid account suspension.

**Q: Can I cancel a booking?**
A: Yes, but frequent cancellations affect your rating. Cancel only when absolutely necessary.

**Q: How do I get paid?**
A: Automatic weekly payouts via direct deposit every Friday.

**Q: What if I have a safety incident?**
A: Use the emergency reporting feature immediately and contact support at 1-800-CHARTER.

**Q: Can I manage multiple vessels?**
A: Yes! Add vessels in your Fleet Management section.

**Q: Do I need my own insurance?**
A: Yes, you must maintain current liability insurance. Platform insurance is supplemental.

**Q: Can I set my own prices?**
A: Yes, you control your pricing within platform guidelines.
