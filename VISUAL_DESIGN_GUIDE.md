## üéØ Pro Tips

### For Best Results:

1. **Stay Consistent:** Use the same colors, fonts, and style across all materials
2. **High Quality Only:** Never use low-resolution or pixelated images
3. **Test Everything:** View designs on mobile and desktop before publishing
4. **Keep It Simple:** Less is more - don't overcrowd designs
5. **Use White Space:** Give elements room to breathe
6. **Strong CTAs:** Every design should have a clear call-to-action
7. **Brand Recognition:** Your logo should be visible but not overwhelming
8. **Update Regularly:** Refresh seasonal graphics and promotions

### Common Mistakes to Avoid:

‚ùå Using too many fonts (stick to 2-3 max)
‚ùå Low contrast text (hard to read)
‚ùå Stretching or distorting images
‚ùå Forgetting mobile optimization
‚ùå Overusing stock photo clich√©s
‚ùå Inconsistent branding
‚ùå Missing contact information
‚ùå No clear call-to-action
