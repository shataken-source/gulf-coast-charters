## ðŸ“Š ADVERTISING SPECS & BEST PRACTICES

### Google Ads:
- **File Format:** PNG or JPG
- **Max File Size:** 150KB
- **Color Mode:** RGB
- **Resolution:** 72 DPI
- **Ad Rotation:** 3-5 variations per size
- **A/B Testing:** Test weather vs community messaging

### Facebook/Instagram Ads:
- **File Format:** PNG or JPG
- **Max File Size:** 5MB (30MB for video)
- **Aspect Ratios:** 1:1 (square), 4:5 (portrait), 9:16 (story)
- **Text Limit:** Max 20% of image
- **CTA Buttons:** "Learn More," "Sign Up," "Download"

### Social Media Posts:
- **Optimal Times:** 
  - Facebook: 1-4 PM weekdays
  - Instagram: 11 AM - 2 PM, 7-9 PM
  - Twitter: 9 AM - 4 PM
- **Hashtag Strategy:** 5-10 relevant hashtags
- **Posting Frequency:** 1-2 posts per day + 3-5 stories
- **Engagement:** Respond to comments within 1 hour
