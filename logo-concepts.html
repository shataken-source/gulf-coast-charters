ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Working with Designers

  • Show examples: Screenshot your favorite logo concept above and share it
  • Specify colors: Use the brand colors from your Visual Design Guide (#0077be, #00a8e8)
  • Request revisions: Most designers offer 2-3 revision rounds included
  • Get source files: Always ask for editable files (.ai, .svg, .psd) so you can modify later
  • Check their portfolio: Look for designers who have done marine/nautical logos before
  • Communicate clearly: Describe your brand personality (professional, fun, adventurous, etc.)
